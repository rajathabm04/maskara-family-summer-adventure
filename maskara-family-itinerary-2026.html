Don't Forget

VISA INFO

🇲🇾 Malaysia

✅ Visa on Arrival
  • Indians get Visa on Arrival — no advance application needed
  • Valid for 30 days
  • Available at KLIA on arrival
  • Fee: Approx RM 200–300 (₹3,500–5,000)
  • Keep passport valid for 6+ months
  • Covers KL, Klang, AND Langkawi

🇸🇬 Singapore

⚠️ Visa Required
  • Indians need a visa — apply before travel
  • e-Visa available via ICA website or VFS Singapore
  • Apply at least 2–3 weeks before travel
  • Documents: passport, photos, bank statement, itinerary
  • Fee: ~SGD 30 per person (~₹1,800)
  • Processing: 3–5 working days typically
  • VFS Singapore office: Mumbai & Delhi available
